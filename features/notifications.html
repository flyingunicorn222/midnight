Notifications

Notifactions happens when:

  • an Order is filled

    @telebroker_bot will you the fill price

  • a Trade is copied

    @telebroker_bot will tell you also your margin, leverage and send you a link to see the trade on 1Broker's website

  • a Position is closed

    @telebroker_bot will tell you also your final profit/ loss and profit/loss percentage

You will then receive push notifications on your mobile or desktop, any platform you running Telegram!

( unless you have notifications muted )
